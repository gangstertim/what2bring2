What is What2Bring?

What2Bring makes event planning easy.

Suppose you're Alice, and you want to plan a tea party. Your table is only big enough for three guests, but you want to invite everyone in Wonderland. Also, you need somebody to bring crumpets, somebody to bring cake, and somebody to bring the tea!
